## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Theme banner uses vibrant brand blue-purple visual style
The system SHALL render the top theme banner with a vibrant brand blue-purple gradient style that feels more dynamic and visually attractive than a flat background.

#### Scenario: Brand gradient is applied
- **WHEN** a core page renders the theme banner
- **THEN** the banner SHALL display a blue-purple gradient style with clear visual identity

### Requirement: Theme banner text remains highly readable on vivid background
The system SHALL ensure the banner text stays clear and readable through high contrast, strong weight, and proper text-shadow treatment.

#### Scenario: Text clarity on vivid background
- **WHEN** the banner is displayed with brand blue-purple background
- **THEN** the banner text SHALL remain clearly legible and visually prominent

### Requirement: Banner keeps homepage-like hero impression
The system SHALL preserve centered alignment, enlarged headline scale, and card-like depth so the first-screen impression feels like a homepage theme section.

#### Scenario: Homepage theme impression
- **WHEN** users open the page
- **THEN** the banner SHALL appear centered, larger than section titles, and with card depth cues (shadow/rounded container)
